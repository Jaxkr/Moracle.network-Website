Services

Consulting and development for your innovative projects

Integrated blockchain apps

Moracle offers powerful APIs for connecting centralized systems to decentralized blockchain applications. We build valuable blockchain solutions that combine the best of decentralized and centralized solutions. 

Full service development

Got a great idea that want incredible reliability, immutability, or transparency? A blockchain solution may be right for you. We provide comprehensive development and consulting services for your business. 

Reliable managed hosting

Managing production databases, application servers, and networks of blockchain nodes can be difficult and time consuming. Moracle provides managed hosting for every component of your solution. 

About

    Continued development and development of experience in the Lisk blockchain space. Developed on every release of the Lisk SDK from the earliest alpha.
