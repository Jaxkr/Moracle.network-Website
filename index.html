An important update for StellarDynamic voters regarding Moracle token (MRCL) airdrops

Lisk has recently completed the mainchain migration to Lisk Core 3.0! We'd like to congratulate the community and the Lisk Foundation on the successful transition. Now that Lisk's voting system has been revamped, we'd like to clarify how your support of StellarDynamic will earn you rewards on our future sidechain.

First, all existing rewards will be honored. Voters who supported StellarDynamic from Sept 1st 2017 to September 20th 2019 will receive 45 MRCL tokens for each LSK they were awarded through the delegate revenue share.

As delegate reward sharing will not play as large a factor in the new version of Lisk Core, we're changing to reward structure to incentive voters. You will now be awarded based on your total vote weight contributed towards the StellarDynamic delegate. Every full month you vote, you will receive 1.2% of your total staked LSK as MRCL tokens.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me on Discord at Jax#4739. Stay tuned for the release of our developer library for Lisk SDK v5!
